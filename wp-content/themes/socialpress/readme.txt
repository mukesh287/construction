SocialPress is a responsive and SEO friendly forum, community, and chat theme. It handles every need you might have as a community website. BuddyPress, BBPress, BuddyBoss, LearnPress, GamiPress and most other social network & LMS plugins are fully integrated into the theme. Use it to create a GDPR compliant alternative to Facebook groups by moving your chat to a website. You can use it to create an Intranet, extranet, and private or public community. You can also create a free or paid membership website with it. SocialPress has a beautiful blog where you can publish news, updates, and other status messages to your visitors. It's SEO optimized (Search engine optimized) and has a fast loading time. The design is clean and beautiful; it comes with a bunch of widget areas you can use to insert Google Adsense or other types of ads to monetize your website. The theme comes with page builder integration (Beaver Builder, Elementor, and Brizy) which you can use to create landing pages, about us pages and other business related content. It works on every mobile device, such as Apple iOs iPhone, Android & Windows phones. SocialPress works with WooCommerce, Stripe, PayPal and Easy Digital Downloads as well which allows you to monetize your website. Last but not least this is a very beginner friendly theme that is easy to get started with and set up.
